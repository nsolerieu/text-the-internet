👋 Hi there

it's weird

but hear me out...

I've designed lots of conversational UI over the years

mimicking mobile native UI on the web is not new. Nowadays there is a ton of ultra sophisticated frontend tools that allow you to build this, and a million other things. Dependencies, complex setup, hard to truly customize... overkill.

that's why I made this tiny thing

one file 🖖

just HTML and CSS

Dark mode

Text, emojis, links, images

That's it

The idea is to simply tell a story like if you were texting a friend, on a webpage

I think it's cool

(of course it's responsive)

more philosophically, I believe that most websites are just people and company trying to put their voice out there. The web has weird standards and websites are trying to seduce us. What if you told your story in this format instead. What would it look like? Would it be convincing?

You can try it in 2min

It's Open Source on github here

Download the index.html, change the content, open in a browser...

Tada 🎉

Now you can text the whole internet

Remember: with great power comes great responsabilities
